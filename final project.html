About us

We are two good friends who finally wanted to move something! We care a lot about the environment and climate change, steadily trying to make our CO2-footprint as small as possible. In the face of climate change we knew, we had to do something.

More information on the climate emergency can be found on the website of the "International Panel for Climate Change", IPCC.

When we collected garbage the first time in March 2021, we knew we wanted continue. The feeling of satisfaction was amazing! Since then we meet every second week - sometimes we grab friends and sometimes afterwards we grab a beer. Usually we go and clean a small area of a park in two hours - and have two full bags of garbage.

Where and when can I join?

We are located in Berlin, Germany. If you want to support us, you have to live around the Brandenburg/Berlin area. But you can always collect garbage yourself - take a friend or set up a local group and go! If you want more info on how to get started, feel free to contact us.
